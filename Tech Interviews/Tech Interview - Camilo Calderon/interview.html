Introduction

I interviewed Hector Calderón who is my father.He is approximatly 53 years young(I couldnt really tell you, I dont pay attention to age). He is a educator and activist for student rights and frequently uses technology to help deliver his message.

Interview

  1. How old were you when you got your first computer?

    2. I was 26.

  2. What was your favorite piece of technology when they were growing up?

    3. The walkman, I could finally have a soundtrack to my life rather than waiting till I get home to listen to music

  3. How has technology changed over your lifetime?

    4. Exponentially faster and more efficient, they started becoming wireless and capable of doings things not thought possible. Also running multiple programs in real time. Putting all items in one(iphone)

  4. Have the changes you’ve seen in technology over your lifetime mostly helped or hurt society?

    5. Mostly helped but at the same time it has made us less social

  5. What is your greatest hope for technology in the future?

    6. It enables us to be more fully human, to connect interact with each other in ways that help us solve world problems

  6. What is your greatest fear for technology in the future?

    7. Technology to be idealized as a god, takes over our governmental systems by virtue of regulations. Eliminations of privacy and weiled as a tool of terrorism.

  7. What advice would you like to share with the younger generation about tech- nology?

    8. Technology is a tool,accelerator but it is not the thing itself. Confusing technology with a solutions where the solution is to have us solve the problems today

  8. What technology would you like to see in the future?

    9. I would like to have teleportation in the future
