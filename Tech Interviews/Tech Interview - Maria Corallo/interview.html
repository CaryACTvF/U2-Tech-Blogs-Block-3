Tech Interview

Introduction

I interviewed my mother, Fedra Corallo. She's 43 years old. She's a real estate agent in New York City.

Interview

  1. How old were you when you got your first computer?

    2. 22 years old.

  2. What was your favorite piece of technology when they were growing up?

    3. A walkman.

  3. How has technology changed over your lifetime?

    4. It does things now that we never imagined to be possible when we were your age.

  4. Have the changes you’ve seen in technology over your lifetime mostly helped or hurt society?

    5. Mostly hurt society.

  5. What is your greatest hope for technology in the future?

    6. That it completely goes away.

  6. What is your greatest fear for technology in the future?

    7. That artificial intelligence will take over the world.

  7. What advice would you like to share with the younger generation about technology?

    8. I would tell people to use it and be familiar with it but learn to not depend on it. Google maps or a calculator are okay.

  8. What’s the scariest thing you’ve seen that technology has done?

    9. It freaks me out how it’s able to track our thoughts, like when you think about something and it comes up on social media. I could talk about my shoulder hurting and what to do for it & the next day I’ll come across doctor recommendations and treatments for shoulder injuries on my feed. I constantly feel monitored.
