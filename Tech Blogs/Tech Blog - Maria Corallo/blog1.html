HUMANS NEED NOT APPLY

Robots Will Take Over

In the YouTube video "Humans Need Not Apply", the narrator speaks about how robots will soon take over all jobs currently worked by humans. Ranging from barista jobs to factory jobs, robots will soon have all the capabilities that humans currently have. The video supports the idea that although robots are emotionless, they require less funding than humans do. Humans require an hourly pay, whereas robots are mainly funded only once and then only require electricity. Humans seem to be becoming useless in the working world because of the advancements that robots present.
